About Me

Following in the footsteps of my father’s artistic hobbies, I couldn’t stop tinkering with pencil and paper as a child. This is how my love for art developed. Of course, children aren’t typically exposed to anything but traditional media, so as I grew into my middle school years, I picked up a drawing tablet and borrowed a digital camera to explore the beauty of digital media.

It was during these years that I started to develop my passion for graphic and web design, and photography. I played around with typography and colors on Photoshop after being awestruck at the work of professionals on Behance, learned how to code to design and develop websites, and took a myriad of photos to document the minutiae of everyday life.

This portfolio website is the culmination of the aforementioned passions. From the works selected for presentation to very the design choices of the website itself, I hope these elements are able to strike a chord with you. Thank you for visiting!
